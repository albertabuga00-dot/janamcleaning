When Should You Deep Clean in Doha?

Qatar's climate makes deep cleaning particularly important. We recommend:

  • Every 3–4 months for regular maintenance
  • Seasonally — especially before and after summer
  • Before/after renovation — construction dust is hazardous
  • When moving in or out — bond-back deep cleaning
  • After illness — disinfection deep clean for health safety
