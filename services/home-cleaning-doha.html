Home Cleaning in Qatar's Climate — Why Regular Cleaning Matters

Qatar's arid climate creates unique cleaning challenges that Doha residents know all too well. Fine desert sand infiltrates homes daily, especially in areas near ongoing construction like Lusail and West Bay. High humidity during summer months promotes bacteria and mould growth in bathrooms and kitchens. Regular professional cleaning doesn't just make your home look good — it actively protects the health of your family.

We recommend weekly maintenance cleaning for most Doha homes, supplemented by a quarterly deep clean to address the harder-to-reach accumulations that standard cleaning misses.
