Our Story

Cleaning Company in Doha — Built Differently

Janam Cleaning & Hospitality Services was founded with one clear mission: to be the cleaning company in Doha that residents and businesses could actually trust — consistently, without excuses.

Qatar's cleaning market had no shortage of providers, but it had a shortage of companies that combined genuine professionalism with transparent pricing and reliable service. Too many Doha families had experienced cleaners who arrived late, used harsh chemicals near children, damaged belongings, or delivered results that didn't match what was promised.

We set out to change that standard — with MOI-registered, background-checked, uniformed staff; eco-certified products that are safe for children and pets; fixed, transparent pricing; and a satisfaction guarantee that's actually honoured.
