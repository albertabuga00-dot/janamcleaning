Villa vs Apartment Cleaning in Doha — What's the Difference?

If you've lived in both a Doha villa and a Doha apartment, you know the cleaning requirements are vastly different. From square footage to surface types, the scope of work changes significantly. Here's what you need to know.

Size & Scope

The most obvious difference is size. A typical Doha apartment ranges from 600–2,000 sq ft, while villas can span 3,000–10,000+ sq ft. More space means more surfaces, more rooms, and significantly more time required for a thorough clean.

Outdoor Areas

Villas in Doha typically have outdoor spaces that require attention: gardens, terraces, balconies, driveways, and courtyards. Apartments rarely have more than a small balcony. Outdoor cleaning adds substantial time and requires different tools and techniques.

Number of Bathrooms

A Doha apartment typically has 1–3 bathrooms. Villas often have 4–7 bathrooms, plus additional guest powder rooms. Bathrooms are the most time-intensive rooms to clean properly, making this a major factor in pricing.

Multiple Floors

Most Doha apartments are single-level. Villas are typically 2–3 floors, often with a basement, ground floor, and upper level. Multi-floor cleaning requires more equipment, more time, and more coordination.

Specialty Surfaces

Villas more often feature specialty surfaces that need specific care: marble flooring, wooden staircases, chandeliers, high ceilings, and extensive window walls. Apartments tend to have more uniform surfaces.

Pricing Differences

  • Studio/1-bed apartment: QAR 150–200
  • 2–3 bed apartment: QAR 250–350
  • 3–4 bed villa: QAR 400–600
  • 5+ bed villa: QAR 600–1,000+
