Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025. At Janam Cleaning, protecting your personal data is a core part of our commitment to you.

1. Information We Collect

When you book a cleaning service or submit an enquiry via our website, WhatsApp, or phone, we collect only the information necessary to provide and confirm your service, including:

  • Your full name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Your property address and access instructions in Doha
  • Service preferences and special requirements
  • Communication history (calls, WhatsApp messages, emails)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data solely for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling and delivering cleaning services you request
  • Communicating with you about your booking (confirmation, reminders, follow-up)
  • Sending invoices and processing payments
  • Responding to enquiries and customer support requests
  • Improving our service quality through feedback analysis

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal information. We may share necessary data with:

  • Our cleaning team members — only what they need to perform the service
  • Formspree (form processing service) — if you submit a form on our website
  • WhatsApp/Meta — if you contact us via WhatsApp (subject to their privacy policy)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. All data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations in Qatar. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with relevant Qatari authorities
