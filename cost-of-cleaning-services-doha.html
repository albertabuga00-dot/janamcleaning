How Much Does Cleaning Cost in Doha in 2025?

If you're searching for cleaning services in Doha, you've probably asked: "How much should I expect to pay?" The answer depends on your property type, size, and the service you need. Here's the complete 2025 pricing breakdown for Janam Cleaning & Hospitality Services.

Home Cleaning Prices in Doha

Regular home cleaning is the most popular service we provide across Doha. Prices are fixed and transparent:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom apartment: QAR 150–200
  • 2–3 bedroom apartment: QAR 250–350
  • 4–5 bedroom apartment: QAR 380–500
  • Villa (6+ rooms): QAR 550–900 (custom quote)

Deep Cleaning Prices in Doha

Deep cleaning covers every accessible surface, behind furniture, inside appliances, and more:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom: QAR 320–400
  • 2–3 bedroom apartment: QAR 500–650
  • Villa deep clean: QAR 800–1,200

Office & Commercial Cleaning Prices

  • Small office (up to 1,000 sq ft): From QAR 300
  • Medium office (1,000–3,000 sq ft): From QAR 400–600
  • Showroom / commercial space: From QAR 500

Sofa & Carpet Cleaning Prices

  • Sofa cleaning (3-seater): QAR 150
  • Carpet cleaning (per room): QAR 100
  • Full home upholstery: QAR 400+

Move-in/Move-out Cleaning Prices

  • Studio / 1-bedroom: QAR 200–250
  • 2–3 bedroom apartment: QAR 350–450
  • Villa: QAR 700–1,000

What Affects Cleaning Prices in Doha?

  • Property size: Larger properties naturally take longer
  • Condition: Heavily soiled properties may require extra time
  • Service type: Deep cleaning costs more than maintenance cleaning
  • Location: Travel time to areas like Al Khor may affect pricing
  • Frequency: Regular weekly clients receive discounted rates
