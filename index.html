Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning services in Doha.

How much does cleaning cost in Doha?

Prices start from QAR 150 for a studio apartment. A 2–3 bedroom home costs QAR 250–350, and villa cleaning starts from QAR 550. Every quote is fixed and transparent — no hidden fees. Get your exact quote here.

Are your cleaners licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Every Janam cleaner is MOI background-checked, fully insured, and professionally trained. We carry comprehensive liability insurance for your peace of mind.

Do you provide cleaning products and equipment?

Yes — we arrive fully equipped with professional-grade tools and eco-friendly, child-safe, pet-safe cleaning products. You don't need to provide a thing.

Can I book same-day cleaning?

Yes! Book before 10am and we can clean your home the same day. Same-day availability depends on your location and service type — contact us to check availability.

What areas of Doha do you cover?

We cover all of Doha including West Bay, The Pearl Qatar, Lusail City, Al Wakrah, Al Khor, Al Rayyan, Al Sadd, Madinat Khalifa, and all surrounding areas. If you're in Qatar, we can help.
