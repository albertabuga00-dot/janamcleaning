How to Choose the Right Cleaning Company in Doha

With dozens of cleaning companies operating across Doha, finding the right one can feel overwhelming. Not all cleaners are created equal — and in Qatar, the difference between a professional company and an informal operator can be significant. Here's our guide to making the right choice.

1. Verify MOI Licensing

In Qatar, all legitimate cleaning companies must be registered with the Ministry of Interior (MOI). This means their staff have undergone background checks and the company operates legally. Always ask for proof of MOI registration before booking. A company that hesitates to share this information is a red flag.

2. Check Insurance Coverage

Accidents happen. Professional cleaning companies carry employer liability insurance and public liability insurance. This protects you if a cleaner is injured on your property or if any damage occurs during the cleaning. Never hire a cleaner who cannot provide proof of insurance.

3. Read Reviews (Real Ones)

Google Reviews are the most reliable source of honest feedback in Doha. Look for companies with a consistent track record of 4.5+ stars and a significant number of reviews. Be wary of companies with only a handful of reviews or those that disappear within months.

4. Ask About Products & Equipment

Does the company bring their own equipment and cleaning products? Do they use eco-safe, child-safe, pet-safe products? Professional cleaning companies arrive fully equipped with commercial-grade tools and certified cleaning solutions. This matters especially for families with young children or pets.

5. Get a Fixed Quote in Advance

A trustworthy cleaning company provides a fixed price before the job starts. Avoid companies that give estimates "starting from" and then increase the price once they arrive. Transparent pricing means you know exactly what you'll pay — no surprises.

6. Look for a Satisfaction Guarantee

Professional companies stand behind their work. A satisfaction guarantee — where they'll return to fix any missed areas free of charge — is a sign of confidence in their service quality.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • No fixed address or physical office in Doha
  • Cash-only payments requested in advance
  • No uniformed staff or branded vehicles
  • Unwillingness to provide MOI or insurance documents
  • Prices that seem too good to be true
  • No online presence or verifiable reviews
